AC Milan

The Club

Associazione Calcio Milan, better known as AC Milan, is an Italian football club from Milan in northern Italy. The club was founded in 1899 and with 18 international titles including 7 victories in the Champions League, AC Milan is the most successful club team in Italy.

Milan has also won Serie A 18 times. With the exception of two seasons in Serie B in the early 1980s, the team has always remained in the first league. AC Milan play their home games at San Siro in Milan.

Zlatan Ibrahimovic

Zlatan Ibrahimovic, born October 3, 1981 in Västra Skrävlinge parish in Malmö, Sweden, is a Swedish soccer player who plays for AC Milan in Serie A and the Swedish national team. Ibrahimović has been awarded the Ballon d'Or twelve times and is generally considered to be Sweden's best soccer player of all time in the Swedish men's national team, with his 62 goals he is the top scorer of all time.

During his career, Ibrahimović has scored over 570 goals and is February 2021, in 22nd place among the leading goalscorers in history. Zlatan has played for AC Milan twice in his career. The first time was 2010-2012 and the second time is from 2020 - now.

Stefano Piolo

Stefano Pioli, born October 20, 1965 in Parma, is an Italian football coach and former player. Since 2019, he has been the head coach of the Serie A club AC Milan. On 9 October 2019, Pioli was appointed as the new head coach of Milan, where he signed a two-year contract. Milan is this season in the lead of Serie A and it could be the first win in Serie A for Piolo with AC Milan.

Team history

Associazione Calcio Milan, AC Milan is an Italian football & cricket club that was founded on 16th December 1899. Milan is the home city of the team and shares with Inter the Giuseppe Meazza Stadium Milan, San Siro as the home stadium which has a capacity of 80,018 spectators . The team's colors are black and red and the nickname is the Rossoneri (red-black).

"We will be a team of devils. Our colors will be red like fire and black like fear, as we will incite our opponents!" said Herbert Kilpin, one of the club's founders.

During their time in the Italian leagues, Ac Milan has won a total of 20 league titles of which 18 of them are in Serie A and 2 of them are in Serie B, but that was most recently in the 1980s. In addition to its successes in the Italian league, the team has also won 19 international trophies, of which the Champions League wins count for 7 of them (the world's biggest football tournament).

In 1908, a conflict arose within the club, which led to a number of members leaving Milan and forming the not entirely unfamiliar FC Internazionale Milano, better known as Inter.

After several decades without a title established Milan as Italy's best team in the early 1950s, largely thanks to the Swedish trio Gre-No-Li. Between the years 1951 and 1962, they took home no less than five titles.
